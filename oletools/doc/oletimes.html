oletimes

oletimes is a script to parse OLE files such as MS Office documents (e.g.Â Word, Excel), to extract creation and modification times of all streams and storages in the OLE file.

It is part of the python-oletools package.

Usage

oletimes <file>

Example

Checking the malware sample DIAN_caso-5415.doc:

>oletimes DIAN_caso-5415.doc
